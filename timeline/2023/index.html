Historical Context

This timeline documents the Gaza-Israel conflict from October 7, 2023, to May 2025, presenting events from a Palestinian-centered perspective focused on humanitarian impact. The conflict began with Hamas's Al-Aqsa Flood operation and Israel's subsequent military response, resulting in unprecedented destruction in Gaza. The timeline chronicles the evolution from initial attacks through ground invasions, temporary ceasefires, international legal interventions, and ongoing humanitarian catastrophe. International bodies including the ICJ have issued multiple rulings addressing allegations of genocide and ordering protective measures, while humanitarian organizations have documented systematic violations of international law. The Palestinian civilian population has borne the brunt of this conflict, with the vast majority of casualties being civilians, including thousands of children, and the near-total destruction of Gaza's infrastructure creating conditions that require years to rebuild.
